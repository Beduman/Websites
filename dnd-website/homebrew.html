Man and machine

The technology that has been recovered and studied the most have to do with biological enhancements, either replacing parts with machine or changing biology to enhance a person in some way. In some areas, this is considered almost vudu and shunned. Each ability has been given a name, something that a person with that ability is called. You can choose one of these for your character to have.

Passive abilities

These abilities are always active, and are never shut off.

Electric conduit "Spark"

Your body has undergone several painful expirements to allow electricity to pass through you. Can be used to power certain things in the world. One person should have this in the party for dungeons and puzzles.
gives you resistance to lightning damage.

Mechanical parts "Cyborg"

An appendage of your choosing can be robotic.
Arm: -1 to constitution and +2 in strength
Leg: -1 to constitution and +5 movement speed

Enhanced Vision "Hawk"

Your eyesight has increased dramatically from either a mechanical part or injections that have changed your ability to see.
Truesight up to 30ft, darkvision up to 120ft

Magic detection "Seer"

Your are able to sense The Weave, and can tell where magic is active or concentrated at, and if someone is a spellcaster
Sense magic and its type up to 30 ft

Active abilities

These powers are controlled at will, and can be turned on or off. These abilities also make one burn up energy, which requires wielders to consume an abnormally large amount of food.
Each use of an ability takes one charge. You'll have one charge + a charge for every 2 levels you are, replenished by eating food and a short rest.

Magnesis "Magnet"

You are able to push or pull metal from your body.
Use a bonus action to push or pull metal up to your body weight within 30ft, requires concentration.
Use an bonus action to push or pull yourself towards metal that is heavier than your body weight within 30ft, requires concentration.
Use a reaction to give disadvantage to attack rolls made with metal up to 15ft.

Solar Flare "Torch"

Your body can use energy to produce light. You can either glow faintly for a long period of time or fill a room with light in a quick burst.
Use a reaction to give an attacker within 15ft disadvantage on an attack roll.
Use a bonus action to produce dim light up to 30ft for an hour.
Use an action to produce light up to 30 ft and dim light up to 60ft for 30 minutes, requires concentration.

Camoflauge "Mimic"

You can turn invisible by changing your skin to look like the background behind you. Can also be used to change your skin, eye, and hair color to make you look like a different person. There are also expensive clothes that change with you if they've been treated to do so.
Use an action to turn invisible while standing still for up to 10 minutes without food, requires concentration.
Use an action to change skin color for 1 hour without food, requires concentration.

Telekenisis "Psychic"

You are able to lift objects with your mind.
Use an action to lift 30 pounds within 15ft without physically touching the object, requires concentration.

Telepathy "Whisperer"

You are able to send thoughts, including pictures, to the minds of others.
Use telapthy up to 120ft.
